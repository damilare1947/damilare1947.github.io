Frequently Asked Questions

Where will lessons hold?

Lessons holds at your most preferred location or at the coaching centre. You either choose to come to the coaching center or request for a private tutor at your convenience.

How does the payment system work?

Lesson costs depend on your location, lesson duration, number of students. You can pay online or through bank transfer when you are ready to begin lessons and have selected your preferred tutor.

NOTE: Payment is to be made before lessons commences.

How do you monitor your tutor?

A progress report form (PRF) is given to every tutor to be signed both by the tutor and the parent or guardian at the end of every lesson period. This is to be submitted to the agency before any tutor can be duly paid. Calls are made directly to the parents to ensure that lessons hold and for feedback.

How do you verify your tutor?

Each tutor undergoes a screening exercise. How do we go about this?

  1. We verify online profiles (credentials).
  2. We verify Government-issued ID cards, conduct interviews: to ascertain their knowledge of each subject.
  3. We verify and contact their guarantors and referees.

What happens if I’m not satisfied with my tutor?

We would immediately replace the tutor or refund your balance in the unlikely event that you’re not satisfied with your tutor.

Global- Sam Coaching Center (GCC)

Global-Sam Coaching Center provides tutoring for both A and O level students needing assistance in any subject area. The centre houses credible, knowledgeable and highly vetted staff who are experts in the subject areas. The centres provides tutoring for children ranging from JSS1-SS3, Adults classes and Remedial Students.
